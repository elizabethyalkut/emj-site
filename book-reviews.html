Book Reviews

I frequently review titles related to plant science for the New York Botanical Garden Plant Talk blog and the New York Botanical Garden Science Talk blog. I also write reviews for the Council on Botanical and Horticultural Libraries newsletter, and am a LibraryThing Early Reviewer.

You can find links to my reviews on my cv.

I welcome the chance to work with book publishers and authors, and am always interested in reviewing for other publications. Most of the books I receive are added to the circulating collection of the Mertz Library at the New York Botanical Garden.

If you are interested in working together, please contact me. Please note that I will only accept physical review copies of a title, as opposed to electronic review copies. I do not accept uncorrected proofs nor advanced reader copies for review, since I review books to add to my library's collection. In the event that a book is not appropriate for any of the outlets for whom I regularly review titles, I will post reviews to LibraryThing and GoodReads from my personal accounts.
