Site Information

This site's design is inspired by my interpretation of the brutalist web design movement meeting web accessibility. I wanted to create something fun, quirky, and user-friendly. Hopefully I have succeeded! The background image is from Ernst Haeckel's extraordinary Kunstformen der Natur (1904) available for view and download through Wikimedia Commons.

Thanks to Rachel Andrew and Grid by Example for Grid patterns and thanks to Koa Kanamee for the headshot.
